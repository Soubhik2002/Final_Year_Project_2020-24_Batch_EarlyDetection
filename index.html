About Project

Enhancing Cognitive Health and Predicting Dementia

Welcome to a transformative initiative that merges cutting-edge technology with compassionate care. Our project is twofold, aimed at bolstering cognitive health and pioneering dementia prediction through the power of deep learning.

Cognitive Game for Dementia Patients: At the heart of our project lies a revolutionary cognitive game meticulously designed to benefit dementia patients. This interactive experience is tailored to engage and stimulate memory, attention, and cognitive abilities.

Dementia Prediction Using Deep Learning: In parallel, our project employs state-of-the-art deep learning algorithms to predict dementia with unprecedented accuracy. By analyzing extensive datasets and leveraging machine learning techniques, we are at the forefront of early detection. This innovation has the potential to transform the landscape of dementia care, facilitating early interventions and improved patient outcomes.

Our Mission: We're dedicated to enriching lives through cognitive empowerment and pioneering dementia prediction.

Join Us: Join our initiative where technology and empathy unite to empower individuals, enhance dementia care, and shape a brighter future.

Game Levels

Level - 1 (Card Matching Prelude)

Welcome to Level 1, where we lay the foundation for your memory-enhancing journey. Your task is simple: match identical cards by clicking on them. Follow these steps to play successfully:

  • Begin by clicking on any card to reveal its image.
  • Take a moment to remember the picture and its specific position on the grid.
  • Your goal is to find and click on the card that shares the same image and position.
  • Continue this process, pairing up cards until you've successfully cleared the entire board.

Level - 2 (Bombshell Challenge)

BLevel 2 introduces an element of caution – some cards are hiding bombs! Your objective remains the same – match cards – but now you must exercise extra care. Here's how to play safely:

  • Start by clicking on a card to reveal its contents.
  • Carefully commit the card's picture and its location to memory.
  • As you search for matching cards, remember to avoid clicking on any that conceal bombs.
  • If you accidentally click on a bomb card, the game comes to an end, so stay vigilant!

Level - 3 (Explosive Grids)

In Level 3, the complexity increases as multiple grids and more bombs come into play. Navigate these challenges with these strategic steps:

  • Begin by clicking on a card within any of the grids to unveil its image.
  • Memorize both the picture itself and the precise position where you found it.
  • Continue matching cards, but exercise caution to steer clear of any hidden bombs.
  • Your ultimate goal is to successfully clear all the grids by matching the cards accurately.

Level - 4 (Card Clash Chaos)

As you enter Level 4, the deck grows, and so does the excitement. Achieve success by following these detailed instructions:

  • Click on any card to reveal its image.
  • Take a moment to memorize both the picture and the position where it resides.
  • Proceed to match the cards as you've done before, but be mindful of the presence of hidden bombs.
  • To conquer this level, maintain unwavering focus and memory retention as you work to clear the board.

level - 5 (Rotation Riddle)

Level 5 introduces a dynamic twist – cards will now rotate like a Rubik's Cube after each successful match. Play like a pro with these strategic steps:

  • Click on a card to uncover it and reveal its image.
  • Memorize both the picture and its original position.
  • As you continue to match cards, be prepared to adapt to the ever-changing rotations.
  • To excel in this level, you must keep your concentration high and effectively conquer this dynamic and engaging challenge.

Level - 6 (Grid Swap Shuffle)

Level 6 brings a new dimension to the game as card grids swap positions. Follow these steps to thrive in this shifting environment:

  • Start by revealing cards through clicks, much like in previous levels.
  • Memorize the images and their respective positions.
  • Continue matching cards, but be aware that certain grids will swap places.
  • Success in this level demands vigilance and adaptability as you navigate the grids' shuffling.

level - 7 (Ultimate Memory Marathon)

You've reached the grand finale, Level 7, which combines all the challenges you've faced thus far. Achieve victory with these comprehensive instructions:

  • Begin by clicking on any card to unveil its image.
  • Commit both the picture and its initial location to memory.
  • Proceed to match cards, navigate the grids, adapt to rotations, and anticipate grid swaps.
  • To emerge victorious in this ultimate memory marathon, you must harness all your skills and cognitive abilities.

Congratulations!

After successfully completing all 7 levels, you've not only completed an engaging cognitive challenge but also significantly enhanced your memory and strategic thinking skills. Well done on your remarkable journey!

FAQs

+

What is the purpose of the cognitive game in your project?

The cognitive game is designed to engage and stimulate memory, attention, and cognitive abilities in dementia patients. It offers an interactive experience to enhance their cognitive skills and resilience.

+

How does your deep learning-based dementia prediction work?

Our deep learning algorithms analyze extensive datasets to predict dementia with remarkable accuracy. By identifying early indicators, we enable timely interventions, potentially improving patient outcomes.

+

Who can benefit from your project?

Our project benefits dementia patients seeking cognitive improvement, caregivers looking for valuable resources and support, and researchers passionate about advancing dementia prediction technology.

+

Is the cognitive game accessible for all dementia patients?

Yes, our game is tailored to accommodate varying levels of cognitive abilities. It offers adjustable difficulty levels, ensuring accessibility and engagement for a wide range of users.

+

How can I get involved in your project?

You can join our initiative by exploring the cognitive game, sharing it with those who may benefit, and participating in research collaborations. Together, we can make a difference in dementia care and prediction.
